# Privacy Policy — WH Roller

Last updated: 13-7-2026

WH Roller is a private tool for coordinating EVE Online wormhole
rolling sessions, used by members of Regrediens.

## What we collect

- **Discord username**: obtained via Discord's OAuth2 identify scope
  when you launch the Activity, used only to show who performed or
  reserved each action (e.g. "Alice reserved a hot pass").
- **Wormhole tracking data**: hole types, signature IDs, pass status,
  and reservations you and other users enter while using the tool.
- **Activity log**: a running log of actions taken within a session
  (e.g. "Bob marked jump 2 complete"), kept for coordination purposes.

## What we do not collect

- No email addresses, IP addresses, payment information, or Discord
  message content are accessed or stored.
- No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

## How long data is kept

All tracking data (holes, reservations, logs) is held in server memory
only, tied to your Discord voice channel session. It is cleared when
the server restarts and is not persisted to a database or disk.

## Your Discord OAuth token

Your Discord access token is used once, server-side, to fetch your
username immediately after authorization. It is not stored after that
request completes.

Questions about this policy can be directed to etip or ingame etip Agittain.
